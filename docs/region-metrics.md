# Regional Metrics

This catalogue defines the regional aggregate fields generated by
`scripts/build_region_index.py` from `public/data/chargers.clean.parquet`.

## Grain

- `state`: one German federal state from `state`.
- `district`: `state` + `district`.
- `city`: `state` + `district` + `city`.
- `chargingUnits`: BNetzA Ladeeinrichtung records. This is not a clustered
  physical site count.
- `chargingPoints`: sum of `charging_points`, i.e. registered Ladepunkte.

City grain intentionally includes the parent state and district. National city
records can therefore exceed distinct city names because names repeat across
regions.

## Scale

- `chargingUnits`: count of parquet rows in the region.
- `chargingPoints`: sum of `charging_points`.
- `reportedNominalKw`: sum of source-reported `nominal_power_kw`; this is not
  delivered energy, utilization, guaranteed availability, or grid capacity.
- `avgKwPerPoint`: `reportedNominalKw / chargingPoints`, rounded to a whole kW.
- `peakKw`: maximum `max_plug_power_kw` in the region.
- `plugSlotsPerUnit`: `sum(plug_slot_count) / chargingUnits`.

## Charging Mix

- `dcFastChargingPoints`: sum of `charging_points` where `charger_type` is
  `Schnellladeeinrichtung`.
- `dcFastPct`: `dcFastChargingPoints / chargingPoints`.
- `normalChargingPoints`: sum of `charging_points` where `charger_type` is not
  `Schnellladeeinrichtung`.
- `normalPct`: `normalChargingPoints / chargingPoints`.
- `powerClass150PlusPct`: share of charging points on rows where
  `max_plug_power_kw >= 150`.
- `powerClass50To149Pct`: share of charging points on rows where
  `50 <= max_plug_power_kw < 150`.
- `powerClassLowPct`: share of charging points on rows where
  `max_plug_power_kw < 50` or missing.

The DC-fast metric follows the source charger type so it stays consistent with
the current operator bundle. The power-class metrics provide a second view based
on reported plug power.

## Connectors

- `connectors`: booleans indicating whether at least one unit in the region has
  each connector flag.
- `connectorUnitPct`: share of charging units whose row lists each connector.

Connector categories can overlap because one charging unit can list multiple
connector types. Do not sum connector percentages to 100.

## Market Structure

- `operatorCount`: distinct non-empty `operator` values.
- `topOperators`: top five operators in the region by `reportedNominalKw`, then
  `chargingUnits`, then name.
- `reportedNominalKwPct`: top-operator share of regional reported nominal kW.
- `chargingUnitsPct`: top-operator share of regional charging units.
- `chargingPointsPct`: top-operator share of regional charging points.
- `topOperatorReportedNominalKwPct`: leading operator share by reported nominal
  kW.
- `top5ReportedNominalKwPct`: combined top-five operator share by reported
  nominal kW.

Operators are exact cleaned names. Brands, subsidiaries, and spelling variants
are not normalized into corporate groups.

## Access And Payment

- `open247Pct`: share of charging units where `opening_hours` is `247`.
- `parking.unrestrictedPct`: share where `parking_info` contains
  `keine Beschränkung`.
- `parking.limitedPct`: share where `parking_info` contains `Kunden` or
  `Besucher`.
- `parking.unknownPct`: share with missing or blank `parking_info`.
- `payment.*Pct`: share of charging units whose `payment_systems` text contains
  the corresponding payment option.

Payment categories can overlap because one charging unit can list multiple
payment options. Do not sum payment percentages to 100.

## Growth

- `firstLiveDate`: minimum `commissioned_at` in the region.
- `newestDate`: maximum `commissioned_at` in the region.
- `addedLast12Mo`: charging units, charging points, and reported nominal kW
  commissioned during the 12 months before the source maximum commissioning
  date.
- `rolloutByYear`: yearly additions plus cumulative charging units, charging
  points, and reported nominal kW.

The current source maximum commissioning date is recorded as `generatedThrough`
in each generated JSON file.

## Not In This Bundle Yet

These require extra source data or a separate methodology decision:

- per-capita or per-household charger density;
- kW or charging points per square kilometer;
- distance/accessibility coverage;
- utilization, reliability, pricing, or delivered energy;
- physical site count from clustering nearby charging units;
- corporate operator-family normalization.

## Map Boundaries

The Regions UI also reads `public/data/regions/germany-states-paths.json`.
That file contains simplified SVG path data for the 16 federal states and is
served as runtime data so the map works under the GitHub Pages base path.

`scripts/build_region_index.py` regenerates it from
`public/data/boundaries/germany-states.geojson` when that source file exists.
If the raw boundary source is absent, the generator preserves the committed
runtime JSON. Boundary attribution shown in the UI is:

```text
GeoBasis-DE / BKG (2025), dl-de/by-2-0.
```
